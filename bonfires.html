Every Friday, Saturday, and Sunday night, from April 1st thru November 1st, the folks at Nana’s Cabana host a Bonfire. Join your fellow campers at the picnic area for a camp wide bonfire. It’s a great way to meet new friends and share fun stories. Be sure to check in at the office to receive your bonfire swag bag, which includes marshmallows, bug repellant, a Mad Libs, and other surprises. Bring your own stick!
